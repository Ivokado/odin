Lasagna Recipe

This homemade lasagna is a hearty and comforting pasta dish layered with rich meat sauce, creamy cheese, and tender noodles. It is a great recipe for family dinners and tastes even better the next day.

    Meat Sauce

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar tomato sauce
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

    • Cheese Filling

  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 1 pinch salt

    • Lasagna Layers

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Extra Parmesan cheese for topping

Directions

Step 1

Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package directions. Drain them and set them aside on a clean surface so they do not stick together.

Step 2

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a baking dish and set it aside.

Step 3

In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until the meat is browned and the onion is soft. Drain any excess fat.

Step 4

Stir the tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, salt, and black pepper into the cooked beef mixture. Let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes, then remove it from the heat.

Step 4

In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, 2 cups mozzarella, Parmesan, parsley, and a pinch of salt until well combined.

Step 5

Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of the baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, then spread some cheese filling on top, followed by more meat sauce. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.

Step 6

Finish with the remaining mozzarella and extra Parmesan cheese on top. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.

Step 7

Remove the foil and bake for another 20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
